ChefBot

ChefBot is a chatbot designed to assist users in finding recipes based on their preferences and dietary restrictions. It leverages natural language processing and machine learning techniques to understand user queries and provide relevant recipe suggestions.

ChefBot can engage in conversations with users, ask follow-up questions to gather more information about their recipe requirements, and filter recipes based on specific criteria such as ingredients, cooking time, or dietary restrictions. It interacts with users through a user-friendly interface, allowing them to input their recipe preferences and receive personalized recommendations.

The bot utilizes Edamam API to fetch recipe data, analyze the data, and present it to users in a user-friendly format. It aims to make the recipe discovery process easier and more enjoyable for users, offering them a wide range of culinary options to explore.
